Kubesail Transition

Dear Kubesail Community,

We want to take a moment to express our deepest gratitude for your support, enthusiasm, and belief in our vision. After much consideration, we’ve made the difficult decision to bring Kubesail’s journey as a company to an end.

While this chapter is closing, the heart of our work—the open-source code we’ve developed—remains available to all. The tools and ideas we’ve shared will continue to grow and evolve, thanks to the incredible community that has always been our foundation. We are proud of the contributions made to the wider ecosystem and know they will continue to provide value.

Our GitHub will remain accessible, and you can continue to connect with us and the community on Discord.

Thank you to everyone who helped make Kubesail what it was. Though our path is changing, the work lives on, with a future shaped by you.

Warmly,
The Kubesail Team
